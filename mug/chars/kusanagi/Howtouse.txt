Paste the lines below to your select.def:

Kusanagi, 
kusanagi/neokyo.def

This will give you 2 versions of Kyo on your Roster namely:

Kusanagi - Original Kyo
Neokyo - KOF2000-2002 Costume

To USE MIX MODE RIGHT FROM THE START:
Press and hold start then press (a) button when selecting Kyo.
Remember that each palette or button you use to select Kyo 
represents a different Mode / Groove combination.

For more info on Groove And Mode check Readme.txt! L2P!
